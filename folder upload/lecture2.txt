unit 1 lecture 2 - Ai terms

Bias is assumptions made by a model that simplify the process of learning.

generative AI is the most common type of Ai that is talked about it is able to create by pulling from other sources and creating its own creations.

Hallucinations in ai is when Ai uses inaccurate or wrong data to display the wrong answer or prompt usually caused by bad data being fed to the AI.

Prompt engineering is the process of making your searches or questions more easy for the Ai or search engine to understand.
 
Temperature when referencing to ai is how creative the ai can be.

Tokens are pieces of data that Ai models use to process information.

generative ai can be summed up very crudely as a very expensive autocomplete
